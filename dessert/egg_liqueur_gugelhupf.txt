5       eggs
250 g   sugar
250 ml  sunflower oil
250 ml  egg liqueur
125 g   wheat flour
125 g   cornflour
2 packs vanilla sugar
1 pack  baking powder

Bake for an hour at 170°C

--------------------------

1. Beat the egg whites to a stiff peak.
2. Mix egg yolks, sugar, vanilla sugar until fluffy, then add the oil and the egg liqueur to it.
3. Mix flour, cornflour, baking powder.
4. Add flour mix to the egg yolk mix and carefully add the stiff egg whites to it.
5. Grease baking form with some additional sunflour oil or butter and fill in the dough.
6. Bake for an hour at 170°C.
